About Rain City Food Share

Rain City Food Share is a non-profit organization based in Greater Vancouver that helps provide fresh and healthy food to people who need it most. We focus on supporting at-risk youth, elders, low-income families, and marginalized communities. Our main goal is to make sure everyone has access to nutritious meals, because eating healthy shouldn’t be a privilege, it should be a right.

Unlike traditional food banks that mostly hand out canned goods, we want to offer fresh produce, poultry, and other wholesome foods. We know that food insecurity in Vancouver isn’t about there not being enough food, it’s about people not being able to afford it.

Our project also looks at what we can learn from food programs around the world, like community food hubs, school meal programs, and food recovery systems that stop good food from going to waste. By using ideas like these, we hope to build a stronger and fairer local food system that helps everyone, now and in the future.
