Our Team

Rain City Food Share was founded by community advocates and nutrition experts who saw the growing food crisis in Greater Vancouver. Our small but dedicated team includes Jenny, Executive Director, who leads our strategic vision and operations; Misel, Volunteer Coordinator, who organizes and supports our amazing volunteers; and Celia, Community Outreach, who builds partnerships with local organizations to extend our reach. Together, we're committed to ensuring no one has to choose between rent and food. We work alongside volunteers and community partners to serve our neighbors with dignity and compassion.
